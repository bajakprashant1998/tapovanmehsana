
# Tapovan International School — Premium Website Redesign

## Overview
A complete redesign of the Tapovan International School website into a modern, mobile-first, premium school website. The design will use an **orange/saffron and blue color palette** inspired by the school logo, with clean white space and warm gold accents. All pages will be built as a frontend-only application with forms linking to WhatsApp/email.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: Saffron/orange primary (from logo), deep blue secondary, white backgrounds, warm gold accents
- **Typography**: Clean, strong headings with elegant body text
- **Layout**: Full-width sections, generous white space, smooth fade/slide animations
- **Style**: Minimalist, premium, trust-building — inspired by top international school websites
- **Logo**: The uploaded Tapovan logo will be used in the header and footer

---

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Banner**: Full-screen with school tagline, "Admissions Open" CTA button, and campus background image placeholder
- **School Highlights**: Key stats (years of excellence, students, campus size, CBSE affiliation) in an animated counter section
- **Why Choose Us**: Icon cards — holistic education, modern infrastructure, safety, discipline
- **Academics Overview**: Grade levels offered with a brief description
- **Facilities Preview**: Visual grid showcasing smart classrooms, labs, sports, hostel
- **Testimonials**: Parent and student quotes in a carousel
- **News & Events**: Latest announcements in a card layout
- **Admission CTA**: Bold call-to-action banner with inquiry button

### 2. About Page
- Vision & Mission statements
- Chairman's and Principal's messages with photo placeholders
- School philosophy and values
- Campus overview with image gallery
- Key achievements and milestones timeline

### 3. Academics Page
- CBSE curriculum overview
- Grade levels (Pre-Primary to Senior Secondary)
- Teaching approach and methodology
- Streams offered (Science, Commerce, Humanities)
- Co-curricular and extracurricular activities

### 4. Facilities Page
- Visual showcase with large image placeholders and descriptions for:
  - Smart Classrooms, Science & Computer Labs
  - Library, Auditorium
  - Sports grounds and indoor games
  - Hostel and dining
  - Transport fleet
  - Safety and security systems

### 5. Admissions Page
- Step-by-step admission process
- Eligibility criteria by grade
- **Admission Inquiry Form** (name, phone, email, grade, message) — submits via WhatsApp link
- "Book a Campus Visit" section
- Fee structure placeholder section

### 6. Residential Life Page
- Hostel life overview
- Daily routine and schedule
- Wellbeing and pastoral care
- Food and nutrition
- Warden and supervision details

### 7. Gallery Page
- Filterable photo grid (campus, events, sports, academics)
- Lightbox view for images
- Video section with embedded YouTube placeholders

### 8. Student/Parent Portal Page
- "Coming Soon" placeholder with a modern design
- Brief description of planned features

### 9. Careers Page
- Current openings placeholder
- Application form (name, position, email, resume upload note) — links to email

### 10. Contact Page
- School address and phone/email details
- Google Maps embed (Ahmedabad-Mehsana Highway location)
- Contact form (links to WhatsApp/email)
- Office hours

---

## Global Components

- **Header**: Sticky navigation bar with logo, menu items, mobile hamburger menu, and "Apply Now" CTA button
- **Footer**: School info, quick links, social media icons, contact details, copyright
- **WhatsApp Chat Button**: Floating button in bottom-right corner linking to WhatsApp
- **Smooth Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover effects on cards and buttons
- **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive design optimized for phones first, then tablets and desktops

---

## Key Design Goals
- Build trust with parents through professional, premium visual design
- Make admissions inquiry easy and prominent across all pages
- Showcase campus and facilities with large visual sections
- Communicate academic excellence, safety, and holistic development
- Fast-loading, clean, and easy to navigate on mobile devices
